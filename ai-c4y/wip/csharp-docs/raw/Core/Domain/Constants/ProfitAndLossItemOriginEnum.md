# ProfitAndLossItemOriginEnum

## Purpose
Defines the authoritative source system or data pipeline for each Profit & Loss line item within BVMS. Exists to maintain a single, strongly-typed contract for P&L item provenance across domain, business, and mapping layers.

## Business Responsibility
Owns the categorization of P&L data ingestion points. This enum is the single source of truth for determining how and where a financial line item originated (e.g., TCO/TCI systems, DA Desk fees, invoice pipelines, bunker/emission modules, laytime claims, voyage/estimate data). Critical for financial reconciliation, audit trails, and routing data to the correct accounting or commissioning logic. Without it, P&L aggregation would lack lineage tracking and auditability.

## Members
- `TimeCharterClaimSystemCreated` — P&L items injected during legacy Time Charter claim system migration or initial creation.
- `TCOInvoiceOwnerExpense` / `TCOInvoiceBunkerAdjustment` — Line items exported from TCO invoices; splits owner operational expenses from bunker cost adjustments.
- `TCOData` / `TCIData` / `TCIOffhireData` — Direct P&L ingestion from TCO/TCI operational feeds, including offhire event adjustments.
- `DADeskFeeData` — Revenue/cost line items generated by the DA Desk fee engine.
- `InternalCommissionData` / `DailyRateInternalCommission` / `PnIRate` — Commission calculations, daily rate splits, and P&I rate adjustments applied internally before P&L generation.
- `InvoiceData` / `ADRInvoiceData` — Standard invoice pipeline items and ADR-specific invoice line data.
- `BunkerCostsData` / `EmissionExpensesData` — Direct bunker cost ingestion and emission compliance expense records.
- `ShipmentCommissionableMiscRevenue` / `EstimateShipmentCommissionableMiscRevenue` — Miscellaneous revenue line items flagged for commission calculation; differentiates active shipment vs. estimate phases.
- `LaytimeClaimData` / `TimeCharterClaimData` — P&L items generated from laytime dispute settlements and standard time charter claims.
- `VoyageData` — Line items sourced directly from voyage accounting and voyage estimate modules.

## Dependencies
- `ProfitAndLossItemEntity` / `ProfitAndLossDtos` — Consumed to populate the `Origin`/`Source` fields on P&L entities and DTOs, enabling UI grouping and financial reporting filters.
- `DaInvoiceProfitAndLossItemsGenerator` / `ProfitAndLossItemGenerator` / `ProfitAndLossItemService` — Used during business workflow steps to stamp the correct origin on newly created P&L items before persistence.
- `FromEstimateShipmentEntityToShipmentEntity` / `ExchangeCurrencyIntoUsds` — Consumed in AutoMapper pipelines and currency conversion utilities to preserve data lineage during DTO/entity projection.
